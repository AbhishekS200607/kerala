◆ Quality Standards ◆

What We Check Before Shipping

Every batch goes through a multi-step quality check before packing and export.

Visual Grading

Each lot is manually inspected for size, colour, and absence of defects, foreign matter, or infestation.

Moisture Testing

Moisture content checked against export-grade limits. Spices dried to safe moisture levels before packing.

Aroma & Volatile Oil

Essential oil content checked to confirm potency. High volatile oil = premium grade — a key metric for buyers.

Curcumin Content (Turmeric)

Turmeric batches tested for curcumin percentage. We export high-curcumin varieties preferred by food manufacturers.

Pesticide Residue Check

Batches screened for pesticide residues to comply with MRL (Maximum Residue Limits) of EU, USA, and Gulf markets.

Packaging Integrity

Bags are checked for secure stitching, correct net weight, and liner integrity before palletising and loading.
