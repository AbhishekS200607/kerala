◆ FAQ ◆

Common Questions

What is the Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?+

MOQ varies by product: Spices start from 100kg, Coffee Beans from 200kg. We're flexible for first-time buyers — contact us to discuss your requirements.

What payment terms do you offer?+

We accept T/T (Bank Transfer), Letter of Credit (LC), and advance payment for new buyers. Payment terms are negotiable for established trade partners.

How long does shipping take?+

UAE & Middle East: 7–12 days. Europe: 18–25 days. USA: 20–30 days. Delivery timelines depend on port and customs clearance at destination.

What export documents do you provide?+

We provide: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Certificate of Origin, Phytosanitary Certificate, Bill of Lading, and FSSAI / APEDA certificates as required.

Do you offer custom packaging or private label?+

Yes! We offer custom bag printing and private label packaging for bulk buyers. Minimum quantities apply — contact us for details.

Which incoterms do you support?+

We support FOB (Cochin / Chennai), CIF, and CFR to all major global ports. We work with trusted freight forwarders for reliable transit.
