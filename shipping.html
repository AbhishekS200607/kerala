◆ Incoterms ◆

Supported Trade Terms

We support the three most common incoterms for international spice and coffee trade.

FOB

Free On Board

We handle everything up to loading on the vessel at Cochin (Kochi) or Chennai port. You take over from the ship's rail — ideal if you have your own freight forwarder.

Ports: Cochin · Chennai

Most Popular

CIF

Cost, Insurance & Freight

We cover cost, marine insurance, and freight to your destination port. Best for buyers who prefer a single delivered price — most of our UAE and EU clients use this.

Delivered to your port

CFR

Cost & Freight

We cover cost and freight to destination port — insurance is arranged by the buyer. A flexible middle-ground option for experienced importers.

Buyer arranges insurance

◆ Process ◆

From Order to Delivery

1

Order Confirmed

You confirm product, quantity, packaging, and incoterm. We issue a Proforma Invoice.

2

Quality & Packing

Products are graded, dried, and packed to export spec in 25kg or 50kg jute / PP bags with your labelling.

3

Documentation

All export documents are prepared — invoice, packing list, COO, phytosanitary certificate, B/L, and more.

4

Shipped & Tracked

Container loaded and shipped from Cochin / Chennai. Tracking details and all documents sent to you.

◆ Payment ◆

Payment Terms

T/T Bank Transfer

Telegraphic Transfer via SWIFT. Typically 30–50% advance with balance before shipment or against B/L copy.

Letter of Credit (LC)

Irrevocable LC at sight accepted from established banks. Preferred for larger orders from new buyers.

100% Advance

Full payment in advance required for first-time buyers on smaller orders. Offers fastest order processing.
